IS THIS THE RIGHT TIME TO SEEK THERAPY?

You may benefit from counselling if:

✓ You feel overwhelmed or emotionally exhausted.

✓ Anxiety, stress, or overthinking is affecting your daily life.

✓ You're struggling with relationship challenges.

✓ You're finding it difficult to cope with a major life change.

✓ You're experiencing grief or emotional pain.

✓ You want to understand yourself better and improve your wellbeing.

✓ You simply need a safe and supportive space to talk.

You don't need to be in crisis to seek support. Sometimes, a conversation is the first step toward positive change.



FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Is therapy confidential?

Yes. All consultations are conducted with strict confidentiality and professional ethical standards.

Do you offer online counselling?

Yes. Both online and in-person consultations are available.

How long is a session?

Sessions typically last between 45 and 60 minutes.

How many sessions will I need?

The number of sessions varies depending on your goals, concerns, and individual needs.

Can I seek counselling even without a diagnosis?

Absolutely. Therapy can help with personal growth, stress management, relationships, emotional wellbeing, and life transitions.
